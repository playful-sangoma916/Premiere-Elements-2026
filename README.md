## ✂️ Editing Basics

You can improve your footage using these standard methods.

*   Trimming: Move your mouse to the edge of a clip on the timeline until the indicator icon appears. Click and drag the edge inward to shorten the clip.
*   Splitting: Select the Razor tool from the toolbar. Click the specific point on your video clip within the timeline to divide the clip into two parts.
*   Adding Transitions: Open the Transitions tab in the sidebar. Drag a transition effect, such as a cross-dissolve, onto the connection point between two clips.
*   Adjusting Audio: Use the audio mixer to increase or decrease the volume of specific tracks to balance the sound levels.
